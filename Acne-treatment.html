Acne Treatment

Discover the best acne treatment products, ingredients, and skin care tips for clearer healthier skin.

Acne is one of the most common skin concerns and may be caused by excess oil, clogged pores, bacteria, hormones, and irritation.

A gentle consistent skin care routine can help reduce breakouts and improve overall skin texture.

Best Ingredients for Acne-Prone Skin

  • Salicylic Acid – Helps unclog pores and reduce blackheads.
  • Niacinamide – Helps control oil and calm redness.
  • Benzoyl Peroxide – Helps reduce acne-causing bacteria.
  • Tea Tree Oil – Natural ingredient with antibacterial benefits.
  • Centella Asiatica – Helps calm irritated skin.

Ingredients to Avoid

  • Heavy oils that may clog pores.
  • Harsh scrubs that irritate active acne.
  • Strong fragrances that may cause sensitivity.
  • Overusing drying acne products.

Acne Skin Care Tips

Wash your face gently twice daily, avoid touching pimples, wear sunscreen every day, and keep your skin hydrated using lightweight non-comedogenic products.
